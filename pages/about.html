What is Calisthenics?

Calisthenics is a form of training, a discpline that aims to explore and refine one's mastery over his own body in space. It requires little to no equipment and is a great option for those who don't want to go to the gym to get fit, as the only weight you will need is your body weight.
Some people also say it is gymnastics for poor people, but that is up to debate.

Did You Know?

Calisthenics is also known as Street Workout. Many advanced movements such as the Front Lever, Human Flag and Planche originated from athletes training outdoors using bars in public parks or gymnasts.
