Equipment Guide

We said earlier that calisthenics requires almost no equipment right ? Well here you can still find some pieces of material that could make your journey/training easier. Hopefully, the small amount of equipment needed convinces you that calisthenics can be done anywhere with a bit of creativity.

Pull-up Bar

Used for any hanging exercises.
Could also be replaced by any surface where you can hang.

Dips Bar

Very useful to do things like dips or planche.
But anything parallel where you can fit in between is fine, even two chairs.

Parallettes

Parallel bar's small cousin. Ideal to save wrist strain on floor movements - However the bare ground can hold you just as well.

Resistance Bands

This is your best friend to warm-up before sets. It can also assist when doing some movements until you are strong enough. Top tier choice if you want to go fancy in your warm-ups.

Walls

Surely everyone has this somewhere at home ? This is a golden piece of equipment that will help stabilize yourself for things like a handstand.

The floor!

Probably the most important calisthenics equipment ! It might be difficult to get access to, but having the floor is a game changer in calisthenics. Strongly recommended to get yourself one of these.
