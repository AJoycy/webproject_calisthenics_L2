Advanced Level

You are now considered a "master" of calisthenics. Even amongst calisthenics athletes you are in the top percentage.

Advanced

Recommended Intermediate Milestones

Here are some skills that you might consider training for since they are now within reach.

Full Planche

A staple calisthenics figure - Challenges your whole body.

Front Lever

The equivalent of the planche for pulling moves. It is impressive to perform and requires significant core strength.

One-arm Pull-up

Taking pull-ups to an even further level. What could be more impressive than lifting your whole body with a single arm ?

Human Flag

We found the answer. Holding your body horizontally might look even better than pulling yourself with one arm.

Training Guidelines

Research

At this point, you should do your research and structure your own personalized training fitting your own goals. Average material available is not enough for you anymore. Self-teaching is the new order.

Structure

You cannot afford anymore a random training schedule and structure. The optimization of your training and approach is what makes the difference now.

Advanced Tip

Innovate, keep extra care of your joints. Do specific joint strengthening workouts - Most athletes get their muscles stronger without improving tendons.
