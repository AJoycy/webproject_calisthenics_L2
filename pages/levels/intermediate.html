Training Guidelines

Quality Warm-ups

Spend an adequate time to warm-up joints and tendons correctly. At your level, injuries could mean months of halt.

Intensity

Each set should be intense enough to require 4-6 mins of rest in-between. That kind of intensity is crucial to build tendon resilience and true strength.

Intermediate Tip

Don't be too harsh on yourself. Progress is not linear, but do acknowledge and reward your small achievements.
