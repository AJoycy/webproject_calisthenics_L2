Beginner

For those still starting. Your focus is on gaining baseline strength and mastering the basic golden skills (push-up, pull-up, dips) that are crucial to progress.

Intermediate

When you start to be comfortable doing the basic movements - This is where you start exploring further things that your body may be capable of.

Advanced

This is where you have enough experience - and strength, to truly move your body in ways the average Joe could not even think of. You are close to overcoming gravity, but the grind can not stop.
